Red Fox

The red fox is the mascot for Marist College located in Poughkeepsie city in New York. The red fox or reynard is indigenous to the Hudson Valley where Marist is located and is regarded as highly intelligent and cunning. It is the red fox from which Marist's school colors of red and white are derived.

Fun Facts about Red Fox

  1. Red foxes have excellent hearing.
  2. Foxes have whiskers on their legs and face, which help them to navigate.
  3. Foxes are generally solitary animals.
